Taco Soup

Taco soup is a quick and easy meal for on the go families. Taco soup is easy to make and delicous to eat!
  1. Gather ingredients:
    • 1/2 pound of ground beef
    • 1 can of corn
    • 1 can of rotel
    • 1 can of black beans
    • Salt and pepper
    • 1 can of kidney beans
    • 1 can of pinto beans
    • 1 can of diced tomatoes
    • 1 envelope of taco seasoning
    • 1 envelope of ranch seasoning
    • Shredded cheese
  2. Brown ground beef in skillet and drain grease.
  3. Add ingredients together in a pot except for cheese.
    • Strain corn, pinto beans, black beans, and kidney beans before adding.
    • Add two cans of water from empty corn can.
  4. Cook on medium for 30 minutes.
    • Occasionally stir.
  5. Place in bowl and add Shredded cheese.
  6. Enjoy!
