Dry Spaghetti

Dry Spaghetti is a stable dish in my household. It is a solid meal option for both single people and families alike.

  1. Gather ingredients:
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • Minced Garlic
    • 1/2 stick of butter
    • 1-2 table spoons of salt
    • Grated Parmesan
    • 1 Box angel hair noodles
  2. Add ground beef to skillet on medium.
    • Add another boiling pot of water for the noodles.
  3. Chop ground beef with spatula
  4. Cook ground beef until brown.
  5. Drain grease from ground beef.
  6. Add salt and minced garlic to taste.
  7. Add butter to mixture and cook an additional 10 minutes
    • Bring heat to medium-low
  8. When water from pot 2 comes to a boil add angel hair noodles.
    • Stirring regulary. Cook 5 minutes
  9. Combine ingredients and noodles.
  10. Plate meal and add parmesan for additional flavor.
  11. Enjoy!
