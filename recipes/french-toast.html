French toast

French toast is the perfect way to start the day. It is a meal that you would be hard pressed to find someone who does not enjoy it.

  1. Gather ingredients:
    • 4 Eggs
    • Splash of milk
    • Bread
    • Cinnamon
    • Vanilla Extract
    • Syrup
    • Non stick cooking spray
  2. Mix eggs, milk, cinamon, and vanilla in medium sized mixing bowl.
  3. Spray skillet with non-stick cooking spray.
  4. Heat skillet on medium.
  5. Dip each side of bread in egg mixture and place in skillet.
  6. Check bottoms for golden brown color.
  7. Pour sryup on.
  8. Enjoy!
